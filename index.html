About

How to pronounce my name: it rhymes with "candy".

I am a Lovett Instructor at Rice University working with Chris Leininger. I received my PhD in May 2024 from University of Wisconsin, Madison from two amazing advisors: Tullia Dymarz and Caglar Uyanik.

Main personality traits: I'm from California, I'm addicted to bubble tea, and I'm always in search of a pool partner (I only shoot 8-ball though, which should be enough information to tell you I am not good).

Research Interests

I am broadly interested in the intersection of geometric group theory, low dimensional topology, and dynamics. I have thought about marked length spectrum, topological, and QI rigidity. My growing list of objects I have worked with include surfaces and their generalizations, right-angled Coxeter groups, and Anosov flows on CAT(-1) spaces. I'm always in search of collaborators- let me know if you're interested!
